Customer details Portal for ISP provider

27 November 2022

The customer data had to be stored securely, and shared across locations at any point of time.

Microzant' Enterprise File store solution helped the ISP provider to store and sharing portal within the enterprise. In a far lesser time we could configure and monitor the client’s file storage. We offered the needful flexibility, as the client could scale capacity up or down as per the need, and also pay only for what is used.

Microzant's online storage provided a simple way to the client to access files and shares them with others. We could also achieve for the client's organization greater collaboration worldwide and imparted an ability to access the files from any internet-capable device.
