EDI Development and Support for a Logistic Giantt

27 November 2022

The client was in a highly competitive market scenario. The exchange of information between different locations had to be fast and timely for better decision making. This created an immediate need for a robust and reliable EDI (Electronic Data Interchange).

Accumenta offered end-to-end and innovative Axway B2B Integration (B2Bi) solutions to the client. This means to adopt a secure, API-enabled approach to EDI that lets you respond faster to evolving business demands. Significantly, Accumenta followed a flexible deployment approach with equal focus on both on-premises and the cloud.

The client is now able to better engage customers and partners across the entire digital ecosystem.
